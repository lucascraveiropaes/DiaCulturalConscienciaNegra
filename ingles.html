Goals to be achieved

The Cultural Poetic Day of Black Consciousness is an event mostly organized by studants of the Federal Institute of Education Science and Technology that aims to uplift, enhance, and spread descendant elements of african culture through artistic presentations. The event is free to all public and has free entrance.

Possible Questions

There will be some way to lunch ?

A: The event won't provide food. However, there will be some classes that will form and will put food stalls to help financial arrecadation of their graduation

Exist transport on the event place ?

A: Yes. Just ahead of the Institute there is a bus stop, where they stop at every 30 minutes

The Competition of Afro Style and Hairstyle happen at the same time ?

A: No. Is there a competition for african hair, and another for african style. Both evaluated separately. If you wants to participate, subscribe here.

How do I do during the event to get assistance ?

A: Throughout the event, there will be volunteers with official identifications, ready to assist any need of the participants.
